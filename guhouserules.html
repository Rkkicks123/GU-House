Rules

Rule #1 (Racism):

The race, religion of a GU member shall not be the topic of jokes, cruel remarks or insults.

Rule #2 (Language):

Always in any GU house public setting, there shall never be any swearing and even racial swears.

Rule Punishments

Rule #1 Punishment (If Broken):

A automatic suspension from all GU activites for three months

Rule #2 Punishment (If Broken once):

A verbal warning

Rule #2 Punishment (If Broken twice):

A suspension from all GU activites for two weeks
