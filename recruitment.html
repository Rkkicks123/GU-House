Recruitment Rules

  • Before appling for Recruitment, you must play the game for more than 1 and a half month's and have proof that you are good at the game.
  • Only people who have OwnerShip can choose to Recruit other people and if the Member does not have OwnerShip the Member must talk to another Member who has OwnerShip About Recruiting the person.
  • Once someone is chosen for a division all of the Owner's have to meet and decide/vote if the Recruit is given the okay.
  • Always the age limit to be a member is 13 years old. Sometimes we might make some exceptions, the only people who let recruits become a GU member are the owners.
  • Once 2, 3, and 4 are complete the Recruit will there forth be a Member of the GU House.
