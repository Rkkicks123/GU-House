Ownership Rules

Rule #1:

Owners can not give or take away ownership with out a vote of the owners and then gets checked by the founder (the founder can veto it and the owners can override the veto with 3/4 of a aye/yes).

Rule #2:

All descions always have any proof that backs the descions up.

Ownership Rule Punishments

Rule #1 Punishment (If Broken):

A suspension from all GU activites for two weeks

Rule #2 Punishment (If Broken once):

A verbal warning

Rule #2 Punishment (If Broken twice):

A suspension from all GU activites for one week.

Leadership Rules

Rule #1:

Only the owners can pick the leaders, but the owners shall vote and then the vote gets checked by the founder (the founder can veto it and the owners can override the veto with 3/4 of a aye/yes).

Rule #2:

There can be only a maxium of two co-leaders at a time for each division and each member who becomes a leader can only be a leader/co-leader of a maxium of two divisions.

Leadership Rule Punishments

Rule #1 Punishment (If Broken):

A suspension from all GU activites for two weeks

Rule #2 Punishment (If Broken once):

A verbal warning

Rule #2 Punishment (If Broken twice):

A suspension from all GU activites for five days
